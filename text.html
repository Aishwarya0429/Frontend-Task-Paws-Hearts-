Our Mission

At Paws & Hearts Shelter, we believe every animal deserves a second chance at happiness. Since 2010, we've been rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to match loving families with their perfect companions, creating bonds that last a lifetime.
